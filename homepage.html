Why you sell your home with Julie Hansen Partnership?

We know when you're ready to sell your home, it can feel like a daunting task. Where do you even start? What repairs and maintenance should I consider? What is my home even worth? How should it be staged? How long is it going to take to sell?

" We want to empower our clients with the knowledge, tools, and belief to achieve their homeownership goals. We strongly believe getting you into the right home can not only help you financially, but improve your quality of life."

Whatever your reasons for selling--relocation, downsizing, growing family--the real estate professionals at Julie Hansen Partnership can advise you on the best course of action to meet all of your goals.

Our Listing Team

It’s critical that you trust the team listing your home. Our listing team has over 30 years of experience in the Greater Richmond market and a track record of strong results. Julie Hansen Partnership sold 137 homes in the past year with great results.

Our founders Julie Hansen and Brett Hansen are involved in every listing, so you can feel comfortable knowing their experience, combined with our teams proven methods will ensure you net the best possible results, while also taking as much stress out of the process as possible.
